# Sazerac
Source: Tabar Inn

## Ingredients

- 1 oz rye or bourbon
- 1 oz armagnac or cognac
- 2 dashes Angostura
- 2 dashes Peychaud's 
- 1/2 tsp simple syrup
- absinthe

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients, minus absinthe, and stir on ice until cold.

2. Rinse a chilled glass with absinthe.

3. Strain.
